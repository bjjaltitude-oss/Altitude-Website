About The Academy

Renzo Gracie jiu jitsu at altitude in Northern Colorado.

Our Philosophy

We believe in tough training and a welcoming room. Everyone is expected to work hard, help their teammates, and leave their ego at the door.

Lineage

As part of the Renzo Gracie network, our jiu jitsu is rooted in proven fundamentals, evolving competition strategy, and a direct connection to one of the strongest teams in the world.

Who We Train

Brand new white belts, hobbyists, competitors, kids, and adults of all backgrounds. If you show up ready to learn and work, you belong here.

The Environment

Clean mats, structured classes, clear expectations. Hard rolls without drama, and coaches who care about your progress and safety.
